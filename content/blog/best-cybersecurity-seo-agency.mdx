## Why Cybersecurity SEO Is Different

The sales cycles are long, which means your content strategy needs to account for a buyer journey that can take 6 to 18 months. Keywords like "endpoint security" and "SIEM" are expensive because competition is fierce among well-funded vendors. And the content needs to pass the sniff test with technically sophisticated readers.

A general SEO agency will write content that sounds good to marketers but gets ignored by security professionals. If you want to learn more about common pitfalls, read about the [7 SEO mistakes cybersecurity companies make](/blog/cybersecurity-seo-mistakes).

## What to Expect from Cybersecurity SEO

SEO is a long game. Expect 3 to 6 months before you see meaningful organic traffic growth, and 6 to 12 months before that traffic converts into a steady pipeline of leads. The upside is that once you build organic authority, the cost per lead drops significantly compared to PPC.

When evaluating agencies, ask for specific SEO metrics from previous cybersecurity clients. Traffic percentages, ranking improvements for target keywords, and lead generation numbers. Vague claims do not count.
