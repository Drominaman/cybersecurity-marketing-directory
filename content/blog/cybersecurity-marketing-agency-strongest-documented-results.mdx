## Why Documented Results Matter

We weight Documented Results at 25% of our overall score because it is the most objective indicator of agency capability. An agency can claim deep expertise, but published results with named clients are harder to fabricate. When evaluating agencies, ask for specific metrics and verify them through third-party sources.

## How to Verify Agency Claims

Ask for references from current or recent cybersecurity clients. Check third-party review platforms like Clutch and G2 for verified client reviews. Look at the agency team on LinkedIn to verify their background and experience claims. Ask to see before-and-after metrics during your sales call.

If an agency only shares vague outcomes like "dramatically increased leads" or "significant brand growth" without numbers, that is a flag. The [best agencies](/blog/what-makes-content-visit-top-rated-cybersecurity-marketing-agency) are confident enough in their work to put specific figures on record.
