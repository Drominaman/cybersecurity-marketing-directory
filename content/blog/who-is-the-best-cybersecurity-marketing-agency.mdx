## How We Evaluate Agencies

Our editorial team scores each agency across five weighted categories.

- **Cybersecurity Expertise (25%)** measures depth of security industry knowledge
- **Documented Results (25%)** weighs the specificity of client outcomes
- **Service Breadth (20%)** looks at integrated capabilities
- **AI/GEO Capability (15%)** assesses readiness for AI-powered search
- **Client Portfolio and Reach (15%)** evaluates geographic coverage and client diversity

We weight documented results heavily because vague claims like "significant growth" tell you nothing. Agencies that publish specific metrics with named clients score higher. ## Choosing the Right Agency for Your Needs

Start by identifying your primary marketing challenge. If you need organic growth through SEO and content, [agencies strong in cybersecurity SEO](/blog/best-cybersecurity-seo-agency) will serve you better than a PPC shop. If you are preparing for AI-driven search, look at [agencies with AI Visibility and GEO capabilities](/blog/best-cybersecurity-marketing-agency-ai-visibility-geo).

Budget matters too. [Most agencies charge $5,000 to $15,000 per month](/blog/how-much-do-cybersecurity-marketing-agencies-cost), but enterprise programs can run over $20,000. Match your budget to an agency with the right service fit rather than paying for capabilities you do not need.

The right agency also depends on your company stage. [Startups and enterprise companies have different needs](/blog/best-cybersecurity-marketing-agency-startups-vs-enterprise) when it comes to marketing partners.
